About

Hello there!

My name is Alexey Kuzmis and I am portrait and fashion photographer based in West London. I developed passion for photography about 5 years ago. I started from street photography where you capture the candid human expressions and feeelings.

It led to fashion photography where I could combine candid street and fashion photography techniques to make sure photos look as natural as possible. My friendly and engaging nature helps to put clients at ease and creates an enjoyable photoshooting experience as well as energetic and captivating final images.

The challenge of capturing the subject’s essence is endlessly compelling to me and keeps me passionate about producing fine photography.

Thank you for visiting my website!
