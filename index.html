About

Hello there!

My name is Alexey Kuzmis and I am portrait and fashion photographer based in West London. I developed passion for photograph about 5 years ago. I started from street photography where I tried to capture the candid human expressions and feeelings.

It led to fashion photography where I could combine candid street and fashion photography techniques to make sure photos look as natural as possible. My friendly and engaging nature helps to put clients at ease and creates an enjoyable shoot experience as well as energetic and captivating final images. I will be honoured to photograpg you and happy to answer any questions you have.

Thank you for visiting my website!
